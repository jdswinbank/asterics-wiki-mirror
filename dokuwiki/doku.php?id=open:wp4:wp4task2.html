ASTERICS WP4 - Task 4.2

Support to the astronomical community

Activities

  • Provision of training (European, regional and national schools)
    • One European School / year
    • Provision of on-line science tutorials
  • Collaboration with science projects to enable them to use the data framework
  • Gathering scientific requirements and feedback

Deliverables

  • First ASTERICS European School (INTA), 15-17 December 2015, Madrid
  • Second ASTERICS European School (CNRS), 15-17 November 2016
  • Third ASTERICS European School (INTA)
  • Fourth ASTERICS European School (CNRS)
